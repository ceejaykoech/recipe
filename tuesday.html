Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, vegetables, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs in a bowl.
  2. Heat butter or oil in a pan.
  3. Pour in the eggs and cook evenly.
  4. Add fillings if desired.
  5. Fold and serve.
